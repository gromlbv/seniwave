Privacy Policy for Seni Wave

This Privacy Policy provides detailed information about how Seni Wave handles personal data collected through our website (https://seniwave.com). Personal data refers to any information that can identify an individual directly or indirectly.

2. Data Controller

The responsible party for data processing on this website is: Seni Wave (Data Controller).

For any questions regarding data protection, you may contact us via email at: seniwave.agency@seniwave.com.

3. How We Collect Your Data

We collect personal data in two primary ways:

Direct collection: information you actively provide, for example, when filling out contact forms or booking a consultation.

Automatic collection: technical information gathered by our IT systems upon your visit, such as IP address, browser type, operating system, and time of visit.

4. Purpose of Data Collection

We collect your data to:

Ensure seamless functionality and security of our website.

Improve user experience through analysis of visitor behavior.

Process your inquiries and consultation bookings efficiently.

Provide personalized communication and follow up.

5. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Our processing of your data is based on:

Consent Art. 6 1 a GDPR.

Contract and pre contractual measures Art. 6 1 b GDPR.

Legal obligations Art. 6 1 c GDPR.

Legitimate interests in maintaining website functionality and customer relations Art. 6 1 f GDPR.

6. Hosting Provider

We host our website content and store related data on secure servers located in the Netherlands. All data collected through our website is securely stored and managed on these servers.

7. CRM and Lead Management

We use Attio as our customer relationship management solution to manage leads, client information, and consultation bookings.

This includes storing contact details such as name, email address, company, and relevant communication data. Attio enables us to track, segment, and follow up with individuals who engage with our services.

Data is stored securely in compliance with GDPR and international data protection laws. We do not sell or share personal data with third parties.

8. Use of Cookies

Cookies enhance user experience and website functionality. Cookies used may include:

Necessary cookies: essential for basic functionality Art. 6 1 f GDPR.

Analytical cookies: require your consent to analyze site usage and improve our service Art. 6 1 a GDPR.

You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

9. Analytics and Tracking

We use Google Analytics 4 to understand how visitors interact with our website. GA4 collects usage data such as page visits, referral sources, and user interactions. We use this information to evaluate and improve the effectiveness of our website and paid advertising campaigns. Data collected through GA4 is processed in compliance with GDPR.

10. Data Transfer and International Processing

Seni Wave is registered in the UAE and uses servers based in the Netherlands. Your personal data may be transferred internationally. By using our website, you consent to this transfer and processing. We ensure that international transfers comply with applicable laws and maintain strict security measures to protect your data.

11. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right of access request information on your stored data.

Right to rectification request correction of inaccurate data.

Right to deletion request removal of your data.

Right to restrict processing limit processing under certain conditions.

Right to data portability receive your data in a transferable format.

Right to object oppose processing under specific conditions Art. 21 GDPR.

Right to withdraw consent

12. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only as long as necessary for the stated purposes, or until you request its deletion, unless we are required to retain it for legal obligations such as tax or accounting purposes.

13. Security Measures SSL TLS Encryption

Our website employs SSL TLS encryption to ensure secure data transmission. You can recognize an encrypted connection by the URL prefix https:// and a padlock symbol in your browser bar.

For questions, comments, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or data protection matters, please contact us at seniwave.agency@seniwave.com.
